Currency Conversion

In Treasure Ledger, your balance is displayed in Gold (G) and Platinum (P) coins.

Currency Conversion:
1 Platinum (P) = 10,000 Gold (G)
1 Gold (G) = 1 Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)

To convert your balance to IDR:

Example:
If your balance is 2P 5,000G:
= (2 × 10,000) + 5,000
= 25,000 Gold
= 25,000 IDR

This conversion rate helps you understand the real-world value of your in-game currency!
